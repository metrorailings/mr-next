DATABASE

All our data is stored on MongoDB's servers.

IMAGES

Vercel Blob is to be used to store and reference all images. Metadata for each image is kept within our database.

GOOGLE REVIEWS

Outscraper is used to scrape all of our google reviews into our database.

E-MAIL

Loops is used to send transactional e-mails to clients and admins.

TEXTING

Twilio will be used for texting.
